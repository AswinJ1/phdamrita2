Why Amrita?!

Eligible candidates shall be provided a variety of research and teaching assistant scholarships. Amrita has a pool of outstanding faculty with Doctorate from globally known Institutions and Universities who continuously engage in research with partners across the world

Frequently Asked Questions

Ph.D. students should focus on networking with academicians, attending conferences, developing teaching skills, and conducting high-impact research. They should also work on communicating their research effectively through publications and presentations.

Publishing the first paper acts as a milestone in a Ph.D. journey, providing direction and building credibility. It boosts motivation, increases visibility in the research community, and encourages further contributions.

Ph.D. students stay motivated by maintaining a research journal, engaging in hobbies, spending time with family, and regularly publishing research papers. Having a clear research goal also helps sustain motivation.

A Ph.D. allows individuals to challenge the status quo, gain intellectual freedom, and contribute to innovation. It also provides opportunities for teaching, learning, and deep research in a chosen field.

To find a dissertation topic, identify an area of interest, engage with researchers in your field, and conduct a thorough literature survey to refine your research problem.

Industry experience before a Ph.D. can be beneficial as it provides clarity, practical exposure, and a better understanding of real-world research applications.

Collaboration enhances creativity, improves problem-solving, speeds up research, and increases learning opportunities. It also helps in gaining recognition through joint publications and interdisciplinary work.

Great Ph.D. students are resilient, persistent, punctual, detail-oriented, and continuously learning. They actively collaborate, stay updated with advancements in their field, and communicate their research effectively.
